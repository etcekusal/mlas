ML ACCELERATOR SUMMIT

An Introduction to Machine Learning and AI





About The Event





IEEE JU SB is organizing its first event of the new academic session. The event is a follow-up of the previous year’s MLAS(Machine Learning Accelerator Summit). It will be an introduction to Machine Learning and AI with hands-on coding, guided by the mentors, followed by a Hackathon. It is about learning from Zero to Hero and creating something exciting in the field of Machine Learning and AI

“ Experience is the name everyone gives to their mistakes ”

So Why wait ? Join with us and attend the best event on Machine Learning and AI and gain more experience to improve your knowledge in ML and get yourself learned by the Heros of ML and AI.







Overview of Event





Machine Learning Algorithms

From this event you will definitely get sound knowledge on Machine Learning Projects. This event's curriculum included different optimization algorithms and how to improve the hyperparameters for faster training model and better optimization. On successfully completing this you will have sound knowledge in ML and AI field that how the projects are organised in Industrial Field and that will eventually help you to carry on further on ML in your future career.



Regularization and Normalisation

This event will be included detailed description on Regularization and Normalisation Procedure to know why these are used in case of training of deep neural network. Also Inverse Drop-out technique and weight initialisation is included in this. You will get a lot of ideas to train your model faster and get better accuracy by tuning the hyperparameters.



Structuring ML Projects

From this event you will get a sound idea about how the ML projects are implemented in the industries and what are the strategies we should have to take as a general recipe of ML projects. In real world tha data comes from very different distribution and that's why algorithm may give error results but proper handling of datas of different distribution will eliminate errors. So Structuring ML Projects is very much essential. After this event you will be able to handle properly the huge amount of datas of ML projects.

IEEE JU SB





The Jadavpur University IEEE student branch, founded in 2010, belongs to the Kolkata section of Region 10 of the organization. We are a group of enthusiastic students who are promoting innovative ideas both within and outside the campus.As one of the oldest universities of the country, Jadavpur University, and its students have a certain duty - the duty to give back to society across all disciplines and inculcate the habit of thinking in every individual who comes along. The IEEE Jadavpur University Student Branch is one little endeavor in that process. One of the most active student branches in our section, we are dedicated to furthering technical and entrepreneurial innovations through hands-on interactive sessions.
